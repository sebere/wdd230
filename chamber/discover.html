Discover Bogota

Gastronomic Experiences

Bogota offers endless gastronomic experiences that allow you to discover new flavors going from the sea to the flavors of the Andes.

Downtown

The city center is full of places to visit, from historical, romantic, bohemian and also family places.

Financial Center

Bogota also has a strong financial system that allows different investments to take place.

Sports

There is a culture of sports, which is reflected in the "Cyclovia" on Sundays and holidays some streets of Bogota become an exclusive corridor for sports.

Sports Events

Sporting events also have a place in this city, soccer is just one example, in Bogota many sports are practiced at a professional level which generate a great spectacle.

Book Fair

For the last 33 years, Bogota has hosted the Bogota Book Fair, a place where lovers of literature can find a whole world to explore.

Theatre Festival

Bogota is characterized by its culture, and this is reflected in the Ibero-American Theater Festival which fills the entire city with culture and in turn allows you to broaden the vision of the world with this art.

Graffiti City

City graffiti is an inclusion project where some city walls become a canvas for urban artists

Religious Tourism

Different religions interact in Bogota and this is reflected in the different religious centers throughout the city, which allows tourism of this type to exist in the city.

Gold Museum

The different museums in Bogota generate an atmosphere full of history and understanding of the past, one of its greatest exponents is the gold museum, which is the museum with the most pre-Hispanic gold pieces in the world.
